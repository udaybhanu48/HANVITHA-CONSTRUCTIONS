About Us

Uday Bhanu Yerramsetty

B.E. Civil Engineering (Project Management)

Uday Bhanu Yerramsetty is a seasoned civil engineer with over 22 years of experience in the construction industry, specializing in residential, commercial, and turnkey design-build projects. His extensive career spans both project management and execution, combining technical proficiency with on-ground leadership.

He began his journey with IVRCL, where he played a key role in delivering several high-value infrastructure projects. Later, he partnered with SMD Architecture and Design, a reputed Dubai-based firm, contributing to the development of high-end residential projects in Hyderabad.

Over the years, Uday Bhanu has successfully led a diverse portfolio of projects—from independent villas and apartment complexes to commercial developments and interiors—always emphasizing quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction.

His approach blends technical expertise, strategic planning, and a strong commitment to client needs, making him a trusted name in the design and construction space.
